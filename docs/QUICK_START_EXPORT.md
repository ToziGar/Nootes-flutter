### Problem: Export fails
**Solution**:

3. Export again and use immediately

**Solution**:
1. Use 3x or 4x quality

3. Ensure graph is fully loaded

**Solution**:
1. Reload graph
2. Wait for loading to complete
3. Check filters aren't hiding nodes
